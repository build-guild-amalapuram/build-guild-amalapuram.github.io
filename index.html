What is Build Guild? +
A 1-day hardware meetup for teens, run by local Hack Clubbers. You'll solder, build, and ship real projects alongside fellow makers in your city.
Is it really free? +
Completely. Hack Club funds per attendee — covering the venue, food, and materials. You pay nothing, ever.
Do I need hardware experience? +
Not at all. We'll walk you through everything from scratch. If you've never touched a soldering iron, that's totally fine — most people haven't.
Who can attend? +
Anyone aged 13–18. You don't need to be a Hack Club member — just a curious teen who wants to build things.
What is Hack Club Blueprint? +
Blueprint is a program by Hack Club — a nonprofit with 50,000+ teen makers globally. It gives teens hardware parts, build guidance, and grants up to $400.
